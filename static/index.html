How it works

From inbox to evidence in four steps

A retrieval-augmented pipeline that never judges without reading your scope document first.

Upload your scope baseline

PDF or DOCX. The document is split into sentence-aware sections and embedded once.

Add project emails

A simple CSV export. Sender and metadata columns pass straight through to your results.

AI compares, clause by clause

Each email is checked against the most relevant scope sections — semantic matching, not keywords, so “squeeze that in” gets caught too.

Review graded alerts

Four risk levels, a configurable alert threshold, and a human decision at the end of every flag. The tool recommends; you decide.

No evidence, no verdict.

Generic AI will happily judge your project without reading your contract. This tool won’t. Every flag cites the exact scope clause it rests on, and a verification layer independently checks that the quoted clause really exists in your document — so you can challenge the machine the same way you would challenge a colleague.

Grounding isn’t a nice-to-have: in the underlying research, the strongest practitioner statement of all was that AI judgements made without reference to project documents would be difficult to trust.

Features

Simple at first glance. Powerful in daily use.

Email-native detection

Works on the channel where informal scope change actually happens — including messages from external stakeholders your internal platforms never see.

Verified evidence

Every judgement carries the scope clause it rests on, plus an independent grounding score. Fabricated citations get flagged, not trusted.

Four-level risk grading

Low, Moderate, High and Extreme — with an alert threshold you control, so the tool respects your attention instead of flooding it.

SMS alerts, deduplicated

High-risk flags can text your stakeholders — once. Alerts carry no email content, so nothing sensitive crosses the SMS channel.

Demo mode built in

Evaluate the full workflow with bundled sample data and zero setup — no API key, no cost, no risk. Switch to live AI when you’re ready.

Anonymised before upload

A privacy scrub runs in your browser: names, contacts and project identifiers become consistent pseudonyms before anything is uploaded. You review every redaction; the mapping never leaves your device.

Audit-ready exports

Download the complete analysis — verdicts, justifications, evidence, grounding scores and model version — as CSV for your project records.
